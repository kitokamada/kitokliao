About

I am a researcher working in astrophysics. My research focuses on the CAMELS suite of cosmological simulations, exploring how astrophysical feedback processes shape galaxy formation across a wide parameter space. I work under the supervision of Francisco Villaescusa-Navarro at the Center for Computational Astrophysics at the Flatiron Institute.

Cosmological simulations — galaxy formation — machine learning for astrophysics — simulation-based inference — neutrino physics — astrophysical feedback
